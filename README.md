# Bakasyonista Photos

This is a simple deployment made for fun!

I believe that part of appreciating a culture is making an attempt to engage with the language, and I wanted to build a small project to assist in doing so!

Bakasyonista means vacationer or tourist in Tagalog.

This tool teaches you common phrases to use while traveling to the Phillipines. 

You can upload photos from moments when you used a phrase such as: "Isa order nang lumpia" meaning "One more order of lumpia, please!". Your photos are available in a travel photo album to then reflect on your trip!
